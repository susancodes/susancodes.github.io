About Me

Born in Myanmar, but moved to San Francisco when I was 9. I love the city lights and that there is always something new to discover at every hill and valley of the city. It's not hard to imagine why people leave their hearts in San Francisco. It sure has mine.

I have a bachelor's in neurobiology, physiology, and behavior from UC Davis.(Go Aggies!) Recently graduated from Hackbright Academy, a highly-competitive software engineering program for women. I enjoy figuring out how things work and love DIY projects so I'm psyched about knowing how to code! My favorite thing about programming is seeing how back-end and front-end interact. It's exciting to see a beautifully structured database but equally important that it has a elegant UI to get noticed.

If you would like to explore the city and get boba/coffee and talk code, please reach out to me!
